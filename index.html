About Me

I am Divya Ananthi, a passionate frontend developer hailing from Kallakurichi, Tamil Nadu, India. My academic journey led me to pursue a Master of Science in Computer Science from Arignar Anna Government College, Annamalai University. During my undergraduate years at Bharathi Women's Arts College, Thiruvalluvar University, I discovered my interest in frontend development.

Fuelled by my curiosity and enthusiasm, I embarked on a journey to deepen my skills in frontend development. I enrolled in a comprehensive course at Login360, Chennai, where I honed my expertise in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
